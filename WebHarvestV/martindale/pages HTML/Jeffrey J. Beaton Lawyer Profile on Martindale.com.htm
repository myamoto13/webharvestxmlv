Biography

Phi Beta Kappa; Jefferson Society. John White Stevenson Scholar. Augustus Todd Fellowship; Goodwin Fellowship; Eastman Kodak Fellowship. Recipient: Outstanding Citizen of Virginia Beach (1992); Russ Walton Award; Honorary Citizen, Hatteras, NC. Author: "The Art of Persuasion"; "Mediating Employment Disputes"; "Social Security Regulations: A Layperson's Guide"; "Mediation: Alternative to Court"; "Taxes and Social Security Disability"; "Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973"; "Federal Law & Disability: A Primer"; "Fair Housing for Disabled Individuals: A Training Manual"; "Housing Tenant Guidelines: Housing for Persons with Disabilities"; Marriage Commissioner (1997). Certified Mediator, Trainer and Mentor Virginia State Bar. Adjunct Professor of Law & Mediation, Tidewater Community College.

 
Reported CasesMelvin v. City of Virginia Beach (EDVA 1997); Zerbe v. Zerbe (EDVA 1994).
